Find. Tap. 2 Seconds. Can you keep up?

2TAP is a lightning-fast color-matching reflex game that tests your speed, accuracy, and focus. Spot the matching colored balls and tap them before time runs out — but watch out, one wrong tap costs you a life!

▸ SIMPLE TO LEARN, IMPOSSIBLE TO MASTER
Three balls share the same color. Find them. Tap them. You have 2 seconds. As your score climbs, more balls appear, the timer shrinks, and every millisecond counts.

▸ THREE DIFFICULTY MODES
• Easy (3.0s) — Perfect for warming up
• Normal (2.0s) — The classic 2TAP experience
• Insane (1.5s) — Only for the fastest reflexes

▸ PROGRESSIVE CHALLENGE
The game gets harder as you improve. Ball count increases from 6 to 12, timer tightens progressively, and difficulty levels evolve: Easy → Normal → Hard → Expert → Master → Legend → Insane.

▸ COMBO SYSTEM
Chain correct rounds to build combos up to x10. Higher combos = massive points. Survive 10 perfect rounds in a row to earn a bonus life!

▸ PREMIUM FEEL, ZERO ADS
Beautiful 3D metallic ball physics, glass-morphism UI, FM-synthesized sound design, and haptic feedback — all hand-crafted for a premium gaming experience. No banner ads. No forced video ads. Ever.

▸ ACCESSIBILITY FIRST
Color-blind mode with shape overlays activates automatically. Haptic feedback toggle. VoiceOver compatible.

▸ COMPETE GLOBALLY
Game Center leaderboards for each difficulty mode. Share your scores with friends and climb the ranks.

▸ BUILT FOR IPHONE
Optimized for all iPhone models from iPhone SE to iPhone 16 Pro Max. 60fps buttery smooth gameplay with zero loading times.
