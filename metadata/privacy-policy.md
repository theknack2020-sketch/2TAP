# Privacy Policy — 2TAP

**Last Updated:** March 16, 2026

## Overview

2TAP ("the App") is developed by theknack ("we", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App:

- Does not require account creation or sign-in
- Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
- Does not track your location
- Does not use analytics or tracking SDKs
- Does not contain third-party advertising

## Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data **only on your device** using UserDefaults:

- Game settings (sound, haptics, theme preferences)
- High scores and game statistics
- Difficulty mode selection

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

## Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center (optional), your scores are submitted to Apple's Game Center service. This is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

## In-App Purchases

The App offers optional tip jar purchases processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information. Purchases are governed by [Apple's Terms of Service](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/).

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children. The App is suitable for all ages (4+).

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
