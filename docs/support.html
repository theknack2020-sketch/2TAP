FAQ

How do I play?

Find the balls that share the same color and tap them before the timer runs out. Three balls always share one color — find and tap all three to score!

What are the difficulty modes?

Easy gives you 3 seconds, Normal gives 2 seconds, and Insane gives just 1.5 seconds. The timer also shrinks as your score increases!

How does the combo system work?

Complete rounds without mistakes to build your combo multiplier (up to x10). One wrong tap resets it. Every 10 perfect rounds in a row earns a bonus life.

Is the game free?

Yes! 2TAP is completely free with no ads. There's an optional Tip Jar if you want to support development.

I'm color-blind. Can I play?

Yes! Enable "Differentiate Without Color" in your iPhone's Accessibility settings, and 2TAP will automatically show shape overlays on the balls.
